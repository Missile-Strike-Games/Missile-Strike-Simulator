Command Manual

▶ Automatic Strike

Select an Origin and Target city. Choose munition type (SRBM, ICBM, Cruise, etc.). Adjust volley size and click LAUNCH STRIKE. Missiles will automatically compute trajectory and attempt to evade air defenses.

▶ Air Defense

Toggle "Air Defense" to activate automated interceptors (Iron Dome style). In Defense missions, you can manually click incoming red missiles to intercept them!

▶ Camera Modes

Use "Free Orbit" to rotate around the globe manually, or "Target Cam" to automatically follow launched missiles to their destination.
